Early Learning Program (ELP)

A time-tested reading-play technique for developing fluent pre-nursery literacy.

Why can children of Deaf adults

read and respond to language

from their parents’ hands

by age 1 year,

while most kids in the USA and elsewhere

                finally read stories from text

                        around age 5 years?

Why did the US Postal Service

        have a machine that could read

        mailing addresses at 12 per second,

                        by the year 1970,

                        while IBM’s Speech Server

                                still wasn’t very useful

                as late as 1987?

Why,

in 2022,

                is it normal for kids

to read their first book

                        three years later

                                at twice the age

                                                that they could?
